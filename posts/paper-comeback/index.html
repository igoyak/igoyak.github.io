Paper Comeback?

May 6, 2025

Small prediction.

I’m suspecting pen and paper as a medium will make a comeback.

Improving image support in llms lowers the threshold of digitizing and should, in theory, allow understanding of structure, layout and non-text.

I enjoy pen and paper as an input medium, but have tried to avoid it since it in my case mostly becomes write-only in practice. But maybe no longer.
